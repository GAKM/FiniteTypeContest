You have been assigned a cost-per-bid of {{ player.COST }} tokens. You will be asked to submit bids for three different contests. The contests will differ in the number of participants and the values of the prizes. Your cost-per-bid will remain the same for all three contests.

All other participants have been assigned a cost-per-bid of {{ C.COST_LOW }} token, or {{ C.COST_HIGH }} tokens, with equal probability. Participants will only be informed about their own cost-per-bid, and not about the cost-per-bid of their opponents.

After the experiment, the final payout for each participant will be determined as follows.
  1. One of the three contests will be randomly chosen.
  2. The participant's opponent(s) for this contest will be randomly chosen.
  3. Given the contest in Step 1, the opponent(s) in Step 2, we will use the participants bids to determine the final payout.
